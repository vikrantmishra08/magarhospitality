The Story Behind MCRPL

In the dynamic world of hospitality, Mr. Yogesh Magar stands as a beacon of innovation and success. As the Director of Magarpatta Clubs & Resorts Private Limited (MCRPL), a company that came into existence in 2003, his visionary leadership has transformed the landscape of hospitality, introducing a blend of luxury, cultural richness, and impeccable service.

A distinguished alumnus of IHM Mumbai, Mr. Yogesh Magar's journey through the hospitality industry has been one of continuous learning and growth. His thirst for knowledge and passion for delivering exceptional experiences led him to further pursue a post-graduate degree from the prestigious International Management Institute in Lucerne, Switzerland. Armed with international insights and a deep-rooted understanding of the nuances of the hospitality sector, Mr. Yogesh Magar embarked on a remarkable journey.

  1. 2003

    On the 26th of October in the year 2003, Deccan Harvest was born. This exquisite multi-cuisine restaurant is nestled in the heart of Magarpatta City, offering a delightful culinary experience that transcends boundaries. Over the years, it has become a beloved destination for those seeking a fusion of flavours and a cosy atmosphere.

  2. 2005

    In 2005, Megameals emerged as a leading name in the corporate catering scene. Serving the top corporate offices within Magarpatta City and beyond, Megameals has been a trusted partner for businesses in need of top-notch catering services. With quality at its core, it continues to be a pillar of support for corporate events and gatherings.

  3. 2009

    The year 2009 witnessed the birth of Cocoon Hotel, a 118-key all-suite business hotel. Burrowed in the heart of Cyber Hub, it has been a haven for travellers and professionals visiting Magarpatta City's corporate offices. Cocoon Hotel seamlessly combines comfort and convenience, making it the preferred choice for discerning guests.

  4. 2013

    On the 15th of September in 2013, Wah! Marathi opened its doors to patrons seeking authentic Maharashtrian cuisine. Situated in Seasons Mall, it quickly became a cherished spot for shoppers and food enthusiasts alike. Wah! Marathi continues to be an all-time favourite, offering a taste of Maharashtra's rich culinary heritage.

  5. 2020-21

    In the challenging year of 2020-2021, MCRPL introduced Megaa Bakes and Megaa Bakes Canteen, its bakery, and confectionary brands. Despite the trying times, these brands have thrived, offering a delightful array of baked goods and treats to satisfy the sweet cravings of our patrons. It is a subsidiary of Megameals.

  6. 2023

    Cocoon Xpress aims to strike a balance between affordability and essential comfort, making travel accessible to a wider range of people. Cocoon Xpress caters to travelers who prioritize value and basic amenities over extensive services or luxurious stays.
